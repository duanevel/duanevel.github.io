Jaquez Reunion Survey #1

What:

The Descendants of Henry and Estella Jaquez and their Families Reunion 2026

When:

September 5, 2026 from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM

Where:

Fountain Creek Regional Park
2010 Duckwood Road
Fountain, CO 80817

Directions:

If you are coming up from south I-25, take Exit 132A. If you are coming down from north I-25, take Exit 132A (Go under the bridge, do not go onto Ft. Carson!).

Go east on Highway 16 for 1/2 mile, then take Security exit to Highway 85 south 1/2 mile to Duckwood Road. Take a right onto Duckwood, then take an immediate left to Pavillion #1 parking area.

Lodging:

There are a few places to stay near the venue including Microtel Inn & Suites, Super 8, and KOA Campground. Further North, off of exit 138 (Circle Dr./Lake Ave.) there are tons of hotels on the west side of the interstate.

Food/Menu:

There are no grills at the pavillion, however they do permit gas grills. Although we have some awesome cooks in the family, we want everyone to relax and enjoy the day. Therefore, we will be catering the event. So far the suggestions are Dickie’s BBQ, Rudy’s BBQ, Qdoba, and Chipotle. I will be sending out another survey in a few weeks regarding your menu preferences. The vendors will likely provide iced tea and lemonade. We will also have bottled water and plenty of soda available.

Music/Entertainment:

The pavillion only has two outlets rated at 15 amps total. The park also states that electricity is not guaranteed. I don’t feel that is enough power for DJ equipment. We would need a special permit for that anyway. But if someone has a portable boombox or karaoke system they want to volunteer, that would be great. Something with bluetooth and/or CD. That way you can play what you like while keeping it at a reasonable volume.

What to bring:

There are 12 metal picnic tables at the pavillion. That’s enough room for 120 guests. If the weather is nice, please feel free to bring lawn chairs, beach chairs, blankets, seat cushions, and sunscreen. It might get cooler in the afternoon, so don’t forget jackets and warm clothing. Also bring your photos, memorabilia, recipes, crafts, and stories. We will have poster boards set up for each head of the family to display your photos and memories.

4. Dogs must be leashed at all times.

5. Please drive/park vehicles in designated road and parking areas.

6. There are a limited amount of parking spaces available and parking along the road shoulder or in natural areas is prohibited. A clear path for emergency vehicles must be maintained at all times.
